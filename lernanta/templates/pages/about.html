What We're All About

Foss Academy is a grassroots open education project that organizes learning outside of institutional walls and gives learners recognition for their achievements. Foss academy creates a model for lifelong learning alongside traditional formal higher education. Leveraging the internet and educational materials openly available online, P2PU enables high-quality low-cost education opportunities.
Learning for the people, by the people. About almost anything.

Our values


Three things guide everything we do: openness, community and peer learning.

Foss Academy is open Being open enables more people to participate and innovate, and makes us accountable. Our community is open so that everyone can participate. Our content is open so that everyone can use it. Our model & technology are open so others can experiment with it and we can all improve it together. Our processes are open so that we remain accountable to our purpose and community.

Foss Academy is a community Foss Academy is community-centered and our governance model reflects that. Foss Academy is built driven by volunteers, who are involved in all aspects of the project. As members of this community, we speak and act with civility. We show tolerance and respect for other opinions, people, and perspectives. We strive for quality as a process – driven by community-review, feedback and revision.

Foss Academy is peer learning Everyone has something to contribute and everyone has something to learn. We are all teachers & learners. We take responsibility for our own and each others’ learning.
